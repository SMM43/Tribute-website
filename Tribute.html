About Raila Odinga

Raila Amolo Odinga is one of Kenya's most influential political leaders. For several decades he has played an important role in advocating for multi-party democracy, constitutional reforms, human rights and good governance. His political journey has inspired millions across Kenya and Africa because of his resilience, courage and commitment to democratic principles.

Leadership Achievements

Democracy

Played a significant role in Kenya's struggle for multi-party democracy.

Constitutional Reform

Contributed greatly to the campaign that resulted in the 2010 Constitution.

Infrastructure

Served as Prime Minister and supported development projects including roads and public services.

Pan-African Leadership

Recognized across Africa for promoting peace, democracy and regional cooperation.

Important Milestones
